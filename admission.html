Welcome Future Students

Begin Your Journey at HSS Larnoo

We are delighted that you are considering Govt. HSS Larnoo for the next step in your educational journey. Our school is committed to providing a nurturing and challenging environment where every student can thrive. This page provides all the necessary information to guide you through our admission process.

Our Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry & Prospectus

Visit our school office to collect the admission form and prospectus. Our administrative staff will be available to answer any initial questions you may have about our programs and facilities.

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit the completed application form along with the required documents. Please ensure all documents are complete and attested as per the guidelines to avoid any delays.

Step 3: Eligibility Check & Confirmation

The admission committee will review your application and documents. Admission is granted based on the eligibility criteria set by the Department of Education and the availability of seats. You will be notified of the admission decision.

Required Documents for Admission

  • Previous School Leaving Certificate (SLC) / Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Marksheet of the last qualifying examination (Class 8th for 9th Grade, Class 10th for 11th Grade)
  • Aadhaar Card of the student
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Category Certificate (if applicable, e.g., SC/ST/OBC)
