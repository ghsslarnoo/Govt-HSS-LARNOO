About Us

A Legacy of Educational Excellence Since 1935

Govt. HSS Larnoo, established in 1935, is a co-educational higher secondary school managed by the Department of Education. Located in the rural area of Larnoo in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir, the school provides education for students from grades 9 to 12. The medium of instruction is English, and the academic session starts in April. The school is committed to providing quality education and fostering the holistic development of its students.
