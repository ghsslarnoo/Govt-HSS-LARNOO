Examination Guidelines
  • Arrive at the examination hall at least 15 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Ensure you have your admit card and school ID with you at all times.
  • Electronic devices, including mobile phones and smartwatches, are strictly prohibited.

Internal Assessment

Your continuous progress is important to us. Internal assessments are a key part of our academic framework, helping us understand your learning journey throughout the year. These evaluations are not just about marks; they reflect your engagement, understanding, and practical skills.

  • Unit Tests & Quizzes: Regular checks to ensure concept clarity.
  • Practical Work: Hands-on application of knowledge in our labs.
  • Project Assignments: Opportunities for in-depth research and creativity.
  • Class Participation: Active involvement in classroom discussions.
