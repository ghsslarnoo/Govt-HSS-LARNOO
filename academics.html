Streams Offered

Science

Ideal for students aspiring to careers in medicine, engineering, and research. This stream is divided into medical (PCB) and non-medical (PCM) groups, with core subjects including Physics and Chemistry.

Arts (Humanities)

Offers a diverse range of subjects like History, Political Science, Sociology, and Economics, preparing students for careers in civil services, law, journalism, and academia.

Vocational Streams

Focused on industry-ready skills, we offer specialized training in Tourism & Hospitality and IT/ITeS, equipping students with practical competencies for the modern workforce.
